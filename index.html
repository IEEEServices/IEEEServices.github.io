--- layout: default --- IEEE Service Hackathon is an event colocated with the IEEE Service 2020 conference that brings together creativity and coding skills.

We welcome different ideas, backgrounds and competences. Developers, engineers, data experts, and enthusiasts from Universities worlwide can participate to the challenge.

Students can team up to participate. Detailed rules will be announced soon.

Students will be paired with some selected volunteer mentors to help develop the project solutions. Please stay tuned for more inforamtion about mentors.

In response to the COVID outbreak, the event will be held virtually.
